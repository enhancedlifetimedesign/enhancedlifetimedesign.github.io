About the Company

We are a start up company operating out of Lassonde B.E.S.T. (Bergeron Entrepreneurs in Science & Technology). We are working to provide warehouse and retail logistical solutions involving innovations in software and hardware technologies. We are currently focused on research and will be offering commercial solutions once we have obtained patents on our technology.

About B.E.S.T.

Bergeron Entrepreneurs in Science and Technology is a start-up incubator operating out of York University Lassonde School of Engineering. It provides an opportunity for students to work on their start-ups by providing space and resources.
